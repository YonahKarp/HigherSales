Analysis

The first step is understanding your business in the context of the industry, category, customers, consumers, sales process, distribution models, and competitive frame. Basic questions beginning with what do you sell? Who do you target? How do you sell? Why would customers buy from you versus the competition?

Assessment and Insights

The next step in beginning to chart the course: defining the internal and external quick wins, prioritizing the pressing issues for short- and long-term success, reality checks, differentiating your products, preparing strategies for execution.

Implementation

With the pieces in place, bringing it from the potential to the actual takes the entire team’s effort, enthusiasm, and importantly, buy-in. Communicating the strategy, gathering the resources, executing efficiently, measuring the results and adjusting brings the vision to fruition, boosting sales and revenue.

Company

Higher Sales Consulting Group was founded by Eli Weinstock. He has managed teams of up to 35 and has been responsible for $100MM in annual sales. Eli has worked with Brands in various industries and categories including Sabra, (Dips/Spreads), Garden Lites (Frozen Meals), Aaron’s Best (Meat/Poultry - Fresh/Frozen), Upper Bounce (Sporting Goods/Outdoors) and Ogrow (Lawn/Garden).

Eli has built and managed sales teams that consistently delivered accelerated growth in both revenue and profitability. His sales work focused on headquarter selling, sales reporting, sales performance coaching, new business development, channel management, territory management, customer marketing and promotion management, compensation planning, collaborative business planning and margin acceleration strategies.

Eli Weinstock and the Higher Sales Consulting team have worked with both traditional and online retailers including Costco, Amazon, Target, Wakefern/Shop Rite, Wegmans, Whole Foods, Kroger, Ralph’s, Meijer, and Albertsons .

Eli holds a BS in Marketing and MBA in Business from the Johns Hopkins University.
